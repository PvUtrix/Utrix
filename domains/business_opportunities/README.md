# Business Opportunities Domain

## Purpose
Evaluate, track, and manage business opportunities including startup ideas, product concepts, partnerships, and business ventures. This bridges between career opportunities and your startup/investment activities.

## Structure

```
business_opportunities/
├── active/                    # Currently evaluating business opportunities
│   ├── [opportunity-id]/     # Individual opportunity folders
│   │   ├── overview.md       # Opportunity summary and status
│   │   ├── evaluation.md     # Business/values/timing analysis
│   │   ├── business_plan/    # Business model and planning
│   │   ├── documents/        # Related documents, contracts, etc.
│   │   ├── contacts/         # Contact information and notes
│   │   ├── offers/           # Your proposals and offers
│   │   └── timeline.md       # Decision deadlines and milestones

├── archive/                   # Completed/passed opportunities
├── templates/                 # Business opportunity templates
├── evaluation/                # Evaluation frameworks and tools
└── README.md                  # This file
```

- Strategic partnerships

### 5. Acquisition & Exit
- Companies looking to be acquired

## Evaluation Framework

Each business opportunity is evaluated across four dimensions:

### 1. Business Potential (35% weight)
- **Market Size**: Total addressable market
- **Competitive Advantage**: Unique value proposition
- **Scalability**: Growth potential and scalability
- **Revenue Model**: Clear path to profitability

### 2. Goals Alignment (25% weight)
- **Career Growth**: How this advances your entrepreneurial journey
- **Financial Impact**: Potential financial returns
- **Learning Opportunity**: New skills and knowledge gained
- **Network Expansion**: Professional network growth

### 3. Values Alignment (25% weight)
- **Fairness**: Fair terms and transparent relationships
- **Freedom**: Maintains your autonomy and independence
- **Sustainability**: Aligned with long-term sustainability goals

### 4. Timing & Execution (15% weight)
- **Current Capacity**: Time and energy availability
- **Market Timing**: Right time for this opportunity
- **Execution Risk**: Feasibility of implementation
- **Life Stage Fit**: Fits current life priorities

## Decision Process

### 1. Initial Assessment (1-2 days)
- Quick evaluation using business opportunity template
- Determine if worth deeper analysis
- Check alignment with your startup/investment thesis

### 2. Business Analysis (1-2 weeks)
- Market research and competitive analysis
- Business model validation
- Financial projections and risk assessment
- Team and execution capability evaluation

### 3. Decision & Negotiation (1-2 weeks)
- Make decision based on comprehensive evaluation
- Negotiate terms if proceeding
- Create implementation plan
- Set up tracking and follow-up

### 4. Implementation & Learning (Ongoing)
- Track outcomes and results
- Update evaluation criteria based on experience
- Learn for future opportunities
- Share insights with network
